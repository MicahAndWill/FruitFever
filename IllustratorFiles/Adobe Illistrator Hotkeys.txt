Adobe Illistrator 



Shapes:

	Hold Shift to make perfect shape
	Press the up and down arrows to adjust how many sides to a shape you want
		- star and polygon


Rotate:
	
	You can rotate a shape with the transform tool or simply by pressing 'r'

Direct Selection tool (White arrow)

	Used to change the points that make up a shape
	you can use shift to select multiple points
